Hi there, I'm Emmanuel Sampson!

I'm a physics and philosophy double major at William & Mary, with a keen interest in technical ethics and the advancement of the world at large.
I have been a writer and photographer for years since I started working for a film production company in high school. From there, I have only improved my abilities in both crafts. Outside of school, I run a digital media and arts outsourcing company providing services to people looking to develop their creative projects.
The future is never certain, but we must strive for the best possibilities we envision. I hope to one day work on greater experiments and projects to bring forth a world beyond science fiction. Each step forward, as well as the expressions of my life, makes me whole.
